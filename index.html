What is Photodynamic Therapy?

Photodynamic Therapy (PDT) is a cutting-edge, minimally invasive treatment that uses light-activated drugs called photosensitizers to selectively destroy cancer cells while preserving healthy tissue. This innovative approach offers hope to patients with early-stage oral cancer and precancerous lesions.
