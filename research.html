Groundbreaking Clinical Trial

Low-Cost PDT System for Early Oral Cancer (2022)

Published: Photodiagnosis and Photodynamic Therapy, JN Medical College, Aligarh, India

Study Highlights:

  • 30 patients with T1N0M0 buccal mucosal cancer treated successfully
  • Portable, battery-powered LED system at 635nm wavelength
  • Smartphone-based fluorescence imaging for treatment guidance
  • Reached rural patients up to 200 km from medical center
  • Complete tumor response with photobleaching correlation

This study demonstrated that low-cost, portable PDT can effectively treat early oral cancer in resource-limited settings.
