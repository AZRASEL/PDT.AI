How PDT Works

Understanding the treatment process

The PDT Treatment Process

1

Photosensitizer Administration

A light-sensitive drug is administered topically or orally. Common agents include 5-ALA or methylene blue.

2

Accumulation Phase

The photosensitizer accumulates in cancer cells over 2-4 hours.

3

Light Activation

Specific wavelength light (635nm LED) is delivered using fiber optic devices.

4

Photochemical Reaction

Light activates the photosensitizer, producing reactive oxygen species that destroy cancer cells.

5

Cell Death & Recovery

Cancer cells die. Healthy tissue heals within 7-17 days.
